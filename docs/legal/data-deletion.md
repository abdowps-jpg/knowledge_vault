# Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: 2026-02-17

## Before Deleting
1. Export your data from Settings -> Export Data.
2. Confirm backup integrity.

## Delete Account/Data
1. In app: Settings -> Clear All Data (local removal).
2. For account-level deletion requests: email support@knowledgevault.app with subject "Account Deletion Request".

## After Deletion
- Local data is removed from device.
- Synced cloud data is queued for deletion where account deletion is requested.

## Retention
Operational logs may be retained for a limited period for security and compliance.
